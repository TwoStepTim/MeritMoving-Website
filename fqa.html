Frequently Asked Questions

Moving Process

You can easily schedule your move by visiting our website and filling out the online form. Once completed, one of our representatives will contact you to confirm the details.

We recommend scheduling your move at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak moving seasons.

If you need to reschedule your move, simply contact us as soon as possible. We will work with you to find a new date that fits your schedule.

Cost and Payment

The cost of your move depends on various factors such as distance, number of items, and additional services required. Use our online quote calculator for an estimate.

A Travel Fee covers the time and fuel required for our team to reach your location. It is included in your overall moving cost estimate.

Payment is typically made upon completion of the move. A deposit may be required to secure your booking, which will be applied to your final bill.

To stay on budget, it's essential to declutter before your move and accurately estimate the number of items. Consider packing yourself or choosing only the services you need.

Tipping is not mandatory but appreciated. If you feel the movers did an exceptional job, a tip is a great way to show your gratitude.
